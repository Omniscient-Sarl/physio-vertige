# Physio-Vertige — Site Summary

## About
Arnaud Canadas is a vestibular physiotherapist based in Morges, Canton of Vaud, Switzerland. The site promotes his specialized practice treating vertigo and balance disorders.

## Target Audience
- Adults experiencing vertigo, dizziness, or balance issues
- Elderly patients with age-related vestibular decline (presbyvestibulie)
- Patients referred by ENT specialists or general practitioners
- French-speaking residents of the Vaud region (Morges and surroundings)

## Services Offered
1. **VPPB treatment** — repositioning maneuvers for displaced inner ear crystals
2. **Vestibular rehabilitation** — exercises targeting balance, eye movements, gait stability
3. **Assessment** — personalized evaluation of vestibular function
4. **Treatment of specific conditions**: Menière's disease, peripheral vestibular deficit, PPPD (persistent postural-perceptual dizziness), neurological causes

## Tone of Voice
- **Professional and medically credible** — uses proper medical terminology (VPPB, presbyvestibulie, Ménière)
- **Reassuring and approachable** — "Je vous accompagne avec une approche claire, rassurante et spécialisée"
- **Clear and educational** — explains conditions in accessible language
- **Direct** — short, concise descriptions, no fluff
- **First-person** — Arnaud speaks directly to the patient

## Site Structure
Single-page design with 5 anchor sections:
1. Hero — name, title, CTA
2. Symptômes — 4 symptom cards
3. Pathologies — 6 condition cards + urgency warning
4. Rééducation — 3-step treatment process
5. Contact — address, email, phone

## Visual Identity
- Clean, modern, minimalist medical aesthetic
- Soft green (#0F766E teal) as primary accent
- Off-white backgrounds with generous whitespace
- One hero image (vestibular system illustration)
- SVG icons for symptoms and pathology cards
- Serif headings, sans-serif body text
