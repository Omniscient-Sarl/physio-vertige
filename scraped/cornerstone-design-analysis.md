# Cornerstone Physio — Design & SEO Analysis

## Visual System

### Color Palette
- **Background**: `#ffffff` (white), `#f1f1f1` (light gray for sections)
- **Text**: `#1a1919` (near-black), `#555555` (gray for links/secondary)
- **Accent/CTA**: `rgb(208, 219, 70)` → `#d0db46` (lime-green), used at 35% opacity for buttons
- **Overall feel**: Clean, clinical, professional. White-dominant with lime-green as the only real accent.

### Typography
- **Font**: Proxima Nova (sans-serif) — used for everything (headings + body)
- **H1**: 48px / 700 weight
- **H2**: 40px / 700 weight
- **H3**: 26-30px / 700 weight
- **Body**: ~16px, color `#1a1919`
- **Note**: No serif font anywhere — mono-font approach. Heading differentiation via size/weight only.

### Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace between sections
- Service cards use a grid with consistent padding
- Article layout: content left (~65%), sticky sidebar right (~35%) with appointment CTA + featured resources
- Hero sections with large background images, overlaid text

## Layout Patterns

### Home Page Structure
1. **Hero**: Full-width image, overlaid H1 "Physiotherapy In Toronto & Beyond", CTA buttons
2. **Second hero**: Photo + text block "Experienced Physiotherapists Helping Patients Since 2008"
3. **Differentiators**: 4-column grid (Quality Care, Deep Knowledge, Customer Service, Award Winning)
4. **CTA section**: "Questions? We're happy to help!" + Call Us button
5. **Services grid**: 3-column grid of all 14 services with short descriptions
6. **Testimonial carousel**: Full-width green background, quotes with dots navigation
7. **Appointment form**: Inline form (name, email, phone, location, etc.)
8. **Clinic locations**: Cards with address + hours for each location
9. **Featured Resources**: Blog post teasers
10. **Footer**: 4-column (Services, Locations, About Us, Resources)

1. **Hero**: Full-width photo, H1 "Vestibular Rehabilitation and Concussion Clinic"
2. **Breadcrumb trail** (implied)
3. **H2**: Location-stuffed heading "Vestibular Rehabilitation in Toronto, North York, Burlington..."
4. **Trust section**: "Why you can trust Cornerstone Dizziness Clinics" with bullet points (credentials, experience, insurance)
5. **Testimonial carousel**: Patient testimonials specific to vestibular treatment
6. **H2**: "What happens during a vestibular physiotherapy appointment?"
   - H3: "Your 1st Session" — detailed walkthrough
   - H3: "Follow Up Sessions" — expectations
7. **H2**: "What is the treatment for a vestibular condition or concussion?"
8. **H2**: "What conditions does vestibular physiotherapy treat?" — bulleted list of conditions
9. **H2**: "What are the symptoms of a vestibular disorder?" — bulleted symptom list
10. **H2**: Fee schedule
11. **H2**: Resources — links to related articles
12. **Differentiator block**: "We're Not Your Usual Vestibular Clinic" (reused component)
13. **Team section**: "Meet Our Vestibular Rehabilitation Pros" — therapist cards
14. **CTA**: "Questions? We're happy to help!"
15. **Appointment form**: Inline request form

### Article Structure
- **H1**: Article title (large, bold)
- **Sidebar**: Sticky with "Request an Appointment" CTA, category tags, featured resources links
- **Body**: H2 sections, H3 subsections, bulleted lists, bold key terms
- **Author bio**: Name, title, credentials at end
- **Social share**: Green circular buttons
- **Bottom CTA**: "Questions? We're happy to help!"
- **Info bar**: "Need more information? REQUEST MORE INFORMATION"

## Content Patterns
- **Long-form**: Articles 1500-3000+ words
- **H2 outline**: Clear hierarchical structure, each H2 answers a specific question
- **FAQ pattern**: Questions as headings, answered in prose + bullets
- **Internal linking**: Links to related services and conditions within article body
- **Trust signals**: Credentials, "Award Winning", "Since 2008", direct insurance billing, private rooms
- **CTAs every 2-3 sections**: Appointment requests, phone calls, contact

## Conversion Mechanics
- **Sticky header**: Always visible with "BY PHONE" and "ONLINE FORM" CTAs
- **Sidebar CTA**: Sticky appointment request on article/service pages
- **Inline CTAs**: "Questions?" blocks appear multiple times per page
- **Bottom CTA**: Full appointment form on most pages
- **Multiple contact methods**: Phone + Online Form always visible in header
- **Trust-first**: Credentials/testimonials appear BEFORE detailed content

## What Makes Them Rank
1. **Condition-specific pages**: Dedicated vestibular rehab page targets exact search queries
2. **Long-form content**: 2000+ words on service pages with all questions answered
3. **Schema**: Yoast SEO WebPage + Organization graph
4. **Location targeting**: City names in H2s ("...in Toronto, North York, Burlington...")
5. **Internal linking**: Services link to articles, articles link back to services
6. **Authority signals**: Team bios with credentials, years of experience
7. **Content depth**: Covers symptoms, diagnosis, treatment, expectations, fees — full patient journey
8. **Resource hub**: Dedicated /resources/ section with categorized articles
